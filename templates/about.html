About Viddash App

Viddash brings file conversion, compression, editing, downloading, and transcription tools into one clean workspace.

Our Mission

We help creators, marketers, founders, and small teams prepare media files without juggling a dozen single-purpose websites. The product is designed to be quick, understandable, and reliable from the first upload.

Every workflow is built around a simple promise: choose your file, pick the output you need, and get a useful result without unnecessary friction.

What We Build

  • Video, audio, image, document, and archive conversion tools.
  • Compression and resizing workflows for social platforms and web publishing.
  • AI-assisted tools for transcripts, captions, and media preparation.
  • Privacy-conscious processing patterns that avoid long-term file storage.
