Common Fixes

My conversion is taking longer than expected

Large videos, high-resolution files, and batches need more processing time. Keep the tab open until the progress bar reaches completion and the download link appears.

The output file will not download

Check browser pop-up/download settings, then retry with a shorter filename or smaller file. If the issue continues, contact support with the tool name and file size.

My video has no audio

Some platforms provide separate video-only and audio-only streams. Use the server-side merge option or convert through the Video Converter page.

Security and Storage

Files are processed for the requested conversion and are not intended for long-term storage. Avoid uploading files that contain secrets, credentials, or sensitive personal records.
