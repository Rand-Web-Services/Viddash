How it works

📹 Hybrid Approach

We first try to extract existing captions from the video (fast and free). If no captions are available, we use AI-powered audio transcription.

🎙️ Better Transcription

Uses OpenAI's Whisper model for accurate audio-to-text conversion. Works with videos in any language.

📥 Multiple Formats

Download transcripts as JSON (with timing), SRT (for video editing), VTT (for web), or plain text.

🔒 Privacy First

Transcripts and videos are never stored. Everything is processed and discarded immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

We support any video platform that yt-dlp can handle, including YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, and many others.

When captions are available, we extract them directly with 100% accuracy. For audio transcription, accuracy depends on audio quality and language. Whisper typically achieves 95%+ accuracy for clear English audio.

Caption extraction is instant (seconds). Audio transcription takes 1-2 minutes per 10 minutes of video on average.

  • JSON: Full data with timing info for each word/segment
  • SRT: Standard subtitle format, compatible with video editors
  • VTT: Web video text tracks (for embedding in HTML5 video)
  • TXT: Plain text, just the words without timing
