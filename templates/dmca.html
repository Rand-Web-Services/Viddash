DMCA Notice Requirements

If you believe content accessible through Viddash infringes your copyright, send a notice that includes:

  • Your name, organization, email address, and physical mailing address.
  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed.
  • The source URL or material you believe is infringing.
  • A good-faith statement that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, agent, or law.
  • A statement that the notice is accurate and that you are authorized to act for the copyright owner.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

Send Notices
