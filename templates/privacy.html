Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Overview

Viddash App provides file conversion, compression, editing, downloading, and transcript tools. We design the product to process files only for the task you request and to avoid unnecessary long-term storage.

Information We Process

  • Files, URLs, output settings, and optional cookies you submit for a conversion or download request.
  • Basic technical data such as browser type, IP address, request time, and error logs needed for security and reliability.
  • Billing, account, and support details if you contact us or use a paid plan.

Files and Cookies

Uploaded files and optional platform cookies are used to complete the current request. Do not upload confidential files, credentials, or sensitive personal records unless you are comfortable processing them through the service.

We may use cookies for essential functionality, analytics, and advertising measurement. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We may use providers for hosting, analytics, payments, email, advertising, and media processing. These providers process data only as needed to operate the service.

Privacy Questions

For privacy requests, data questions, or account support, contact the Viddash team.
