Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions.

GeneralConversionCompressionAccount & BillingPrivacy & SecurityTechnical
Is Viddash App really free to use?

Yes. You can convert, compress, and edit files for free within the current upload limits.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required for the free browser tools.

Is my data secure?

Files are processed for the requested conversion and are not intended to be stored permanently.

What file formats are supported?

Viddash supports common video, image, and audio formats including MP4, MOV, WebM, PNG, JPG, WebP, MP3, and WAV.

What is the maximum file size?

Image conversion currently supports up to 25 MB per image and 100 MB total per image batch. Video upload limits are configurable on the server.

Can I use Viddash App on mobile devices?

Yes. The interface is responsive and works on modern mobile browsers.
